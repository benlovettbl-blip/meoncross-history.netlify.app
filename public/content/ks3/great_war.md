## 3.1 The Alliance System

By 1914, Europe was divided into two rival military alliances, which turned a localized conflict in the Balkans into a global war:

1. **The Triple Entente**: Consisting of Great Britain, France, and Russia.
2. **The Triple Alliance**: Consisting of Germany, Austria-Hungary, and Italy (though Italy later changed sides).

When Archduke Franz Ferdinand of Austria was assassinated by a Serbian nationalist in June 1914, the alliance treaties forced these major powers to declare war on each other.

### Vocabulary
- **Alliance**: A formal agreement between nations to cooperate and defend each other.
- **Nationalist**: A person who strongly identifies with their nation and supports its interests.
