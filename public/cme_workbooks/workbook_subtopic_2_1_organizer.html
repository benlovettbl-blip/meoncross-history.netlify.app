Task 2: Key Vocabulary Match-Up
Historical Term Your Match Definition / Significance
1. PLO A. 1966 Israeli military reprisal raid in the West Bank that escalated Jordanian tensions.
2. Samu Raid B. Palestine Liberation Organization, founded in 1964 to coordinate national resistance.
3. MiG fighters C. Soviet-built jets flown by Syria; six were shot down by Israeli fighters in April 1967.
4. Straits of Tiran D. Waterway closed by Nasser in May 1967, closing off Israel's southern port of Eilat.
5. Operation Focus E. Israel's preemptive air strikes which destroyed the Egyptian air force on the ground.
6. Golan Heights F. Strategic mountainous border region captured by Israel from Syria.
7. UNEF G. UN peacekeeping force expelled by Nasser from the Sinai Peninsula prior to the war.
