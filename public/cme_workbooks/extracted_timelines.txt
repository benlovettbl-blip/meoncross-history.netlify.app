
### subtopic_1_1: British Withdrawal & Creation of Israel (1945–1948)
- ⚡ 1945: Pressures: Holocaust survivors seek entry; US exerts diplomatic pressure; Bevin sets 1,500 quota.
- 💥 1946-47: Insurgency: King David Hotel bombed (91 deaths); Irgun hangs two British sergeants, breaking UK domestic resolve.
- ⚖️ 1947: UN Referral: SS Exodus turned away; Britain hands dilemma to UN; Resolution 181 partition plan passes.
- 🏁 1948-49: Outbreak: British forces withdraw; State of Israel declared; Arab armies invade; IDF victory results in Nakba.

### subtopic_1_2: The Aftermath of the 1948–49 War
- ⚡ 1949: Armistice & Nakba: Armistice agreements establish the Green Line. 700,000 Palestinian refugees displaced during the Nakba; UNRWA founded.
- 📜 1950: Law of Return: Israel passes the Law of Return; population doubles in three years; Tzena austerity and rationing introduced.
- 💥 1951-55: Fedayeen Cycle: Palestinian Fedayeen launch raids from Gaza; IDF responds with reprisal raids. Egypt blockades Suez and Straits of Tiran.
- 🇺🇸 1950s: Superpower Aid: US grants $300 million in financial aid to prevent Israel's economic collapse. Count Bernadotte assassinated.

### subtopic_1_3: The Suez Crisis (1956)
- ⚡ Sept 1955: Czech Arms Deal: Egypt signs Soviet arms deal; regional military balance shifts.
- 🌊 July 1956: Nationalisation: Nasser nationalizes Suez Canal to fund Aswan Dam after Western loans fail.
- ⚔️ Oct 1956: Sinai Invasion: Israel invades Sinai; UK/France land paratroopers under secret Sèvres pact.
- 🏁 Nov 1956: Superpower Halts: US economic pressure forces withdrawal; UNEF deployed; Nasser becomes hero.

### subtopic_2_1: The Six Day War (1967)
- 💧 1964: Cairo Conference: Arab League founds PLO; Jordan water diversion plan begins.
- 💥 Nov 1966: Samu Raid: Israeli reprisal in Jordan; escalates West Bank/Jordan tensions.
- 🛡️ May 1967: Mobilization: UNEF expelled; Nasser blockades Tiran; Soviet false warning.
- 🏁 June 1967: Six Day War: Israel launches preemptive strikes; captures Sinai, Gaza, West Bank, Golan.

### subtopic_2_2: Occupied Territories & Palestinian Militancy (1967–73)
- ⚖️ Nov 1967: Resolution 242: UN passes 'Land for Peace' framework; Khartoum Conference rejects it with 'Three Nos'.
- 🌊 1967-70: War of Attrition: Artillery exchanges along Suez; early Israeli settlements begin in West Bank.
- ✈️ Sept 1970: Dawson's Field: PFLP hijacks planes; King Hussein launches Black September civil war to expel PLO.
- 🏅 Sept 1972: Munich Massacre: Black September kills 11 Israeli athletes; Mossad launches Operation Wrath of God.

### subtopic_2_3: The Yom Kippur War (1973)
- 🛡️ 1972: Soviet Expulsion: Sadat expels 15,000 Soviet advisors to signal US openness.
- ⚔️ 6 Oct 1973: Surprise Attack: Egyptian/Syrian forces cross Suez/Golan on Yom Kippur.
- 🛢️ Oct 1973: OPEC Embargo: Oil production cuts and embargo trigger global energy crisis.
- 🏁 Oct 1973: Ceasefire: Resolution 338; Israeli forces cross Suez; shuttle diplomacy starts.

### subtopic_3_1: Camp David & Egypt-Israel Peace (1974–79)
- ✈️ 1974-75: Shuttle Diplomacy: Kissinger brokers troop disengagements; Suez Canal reopens to global trade.
- 🤝 Nov 1977: Knesset Visit: Sadat flies to Jerusalem, speaking to Israeli parliament, breaking diplomatic stalemate.
- 🏡 Sept 1978: Camp David: Carter hosts Sadat and Begin; frameworks for peace and Palestinian autonomy signed.
- 🏁 March 1979: Washington Treaty: Formal peace treaty signed; Israel returns Sinai; Egypt expelled from Arab League.

### subtopic_3_2: Lebanon & the Rise of Intifada (1978–89)
- 🚀 1978: Litani Operation: IDF invades south Lebanon to push PLO back from border.
- ⚔️ June 1982: Lebanon Invasion: Israel besieges Beirut; Sabra & Shatila massacres occur; PLO moves to Tunis; Hezbollah forms.
- 🪨 Dec 1987: First Intifada: Grassroots uprising starts in Gaza; stones campaign against IDF tanks.
- 👊 1988: Iron Fist: Rabin enforces physical force policy; Hamas publishes charter; global opinion shifts.

### subtopic_3_3: Peace Process & Oslo Accords (1990–95)
- 🌍 Oct 1991: Madrid Conference: Post-Gulf War diplomatic summit brings parties together; direct talks begin.
- 🤝 Sept 1993: Oslo I Accords: Declaration of Principles signed; Clinton hosts historic Rabin-Arafat handshake.
- 🇯🇴 Oct 1994: Jordan Peace: Israel and Jordan sign peace treaty, ending 46-year state of war.
- 🏁 Nov 1995: Rabin Assassinated: Right-wing Israeli extremist kills Rabin, freezing the peace process.
