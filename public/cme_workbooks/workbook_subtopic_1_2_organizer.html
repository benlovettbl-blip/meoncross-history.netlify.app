Task 2: Key Vocabulary Match-Up
Historical Term Your Match Definition / Significance
1. Green Line A. Arabic term for 'Catastrophe'; refers to the displacement of 700,000 Palestinians.
2. Nakba B. 1949 armistice borders separating Israel from neighboring Arab states.
3. UNRWA C. UN agency created in 1949 to manage camps and provide food and education to refugees.
4. Law of Return D. 1950 legislation granting any Jewish immigrant the right to automatic citizenship.
5. Tzena E. Strict economic austerity and food rationing system implemented in early 1950s Israel.
6. Fedayeen F. Palestinian guerrilla fighters who launched border incursions from the Gaza Strip.
7. Reprisals G. Disproportionate military retaliatory strikes launched by the IDF in response to border raids.
