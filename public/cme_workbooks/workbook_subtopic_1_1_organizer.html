Task 2: Key Vocabulary Match-Up
Historical Term Your Match Definition / Significance
1. Zionism A. British Foreign Secretary who restricted Jewish quota to 1,500/month.
2. Ernest Bevin B. Movement advocating for a sovereign Jewish homeland in Palestine.
3. King David Hotel C. British administrative headquarters bombed in 1946 by the Irgun, killing 91 people.
4. SS Exodus D. Jewish refugee ship intercepted and forcibly turned away by Britain in 1947.
5. Resolution 181 E. The 1947 UN Partition Plan proposing separate Jewish and Arab states.
6. Czechoslovakia F. Supplied weapons to Israel, helping IDF double effective troops from 35k to 108k.
7. Nakba G. Arab word for 'Catastrophe', referring to the displacement of 700,000 Palestinian Arabs.
